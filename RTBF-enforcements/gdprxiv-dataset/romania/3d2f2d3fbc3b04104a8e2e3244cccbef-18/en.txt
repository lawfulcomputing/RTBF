23.11.2020
Penalty for GDPR violation

The National Supervisory Authority completed on 04.11.2020 an investigation at the Vodafone Romania S.A. operator. and found the violation of the provisions of art. 12, art. 15 and art. 17 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
The Vodafone Romania SA operator was fined 19,468.8 lei, the equivalent of 4,000 EURO.
The sanction was applied as a result of some complaints claiming that the operator did not respond to requests to exercise the rights of access and deletion provided for in art. 15 and art. 17 of the General Data Protection Regulation.
